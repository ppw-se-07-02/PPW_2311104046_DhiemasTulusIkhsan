Wicked: For Good (2025)

Genre: Fantasy / Musical / Drama

Synopsis: Before Dorothy arrived in Oz, two young witches — the misunderstood green-skinned Elphaba and the radiant Glinda — formed an unlikely bond. Their friendship soon faces the harsh realities of power, prejudice, and destiny. “Wicked” reveals the truth behind the legend of the Wicked Witch of the West.

Highlights

  • Based on the iconic Broadway musical “Wicked”.
  • Starring Cynthia Erivo as Elphaba and Ariana Grande as Glinda.
  • Directed by Jon M. Chu, known for “Crazy Rich Asians”.

Review

“Wicked” is visually breathtaking — rich in color, emotion, and harmony. The film redefines the boundaries of fantasy cinema with its powerful performances, stunning production design, and emotionally resonant score. A tale of friendship, identity, and defying destiny.
